Manual Robotics Workshop 2022

Manual Robotics Workshop '22 was incredibly productive, providing participants with hands-on experience in designing a remote controlled car. Following a two-year hiatus, this was the first offline event held. The large number of participants and the requirement that they work in groups of five allowed them to get to know and interact with each other, and imbibe networking and collaborative skills. The workshop's goal was to teach participants how to build a bot from scratch and get it to function. Teams were given the opportunity to compete in an exciting race, where they handled their cars meticulously on the convoluted race track. It was a great learning experience that also provided them with the opportunity to test and develop their creativity.
